Executive Summary Investor View

ResQ is an AI-first emergency response aggregation platform that unifies government (108/102) and private ambulance, fire and rescue units on a single real-time interface. We focus on three non-negotiables: response time, transparency and trust. The product combines a consumer app, driver app and command dashboard to orchestrate the nearest verified vehicle with live tracking and automated family updates.

What ResQ Actually Feels Like

‚Äú108 + Ola + Google Maps for emergencies‚Äù ‚Äî one tap SOS, auto-detected location, AI-picked route, and family members notified in parallel. No more guessing where the ambulance is or when it arrives.

Who We Serve First

Urban families, hospitals, and housing societies in cities like Hyderabad, where traffic and fragmented fleets make 10‚Äì20 minute delays common. ResQ begins with high-density clusters, then expands via hospital + operator partnerships.

Why Now Timing Advantage

India is simultaneously witnessing rapid urbanisation, smartphone penetration and a growing tolerance for app-based logistics ‚Äî but emergency response still runs on phone calls, manual registers and unstructured WhatsApp groups. The stack is ready, but the orchestration layer is missing.

Urban chaos ‚Üí Digital gap Hyper-growth traffic + metro expansion, yet most ambulances are not digitally discoverable or trackable by citizens.
Behavioral shift People trust ‚Äúbook, track, rate‚Äù models for taxis and deliveries. ResQ extends this behaviour to life-saving emergencies.
AI infra is ready Maps, telemetry, ML infra and cloud messaging exist. The opportunity is in packaging them into a specialised emergency layer.

Solution & Experience One Interface, All Emergencies

ResQ is a single experience that handles emergency requests end-to-end: instant booking, AI-optimised routing, live GPS tracking, automated family notifications, and a verified provider marketplace. Designed to be government-compatible while unlocking private fleet efficiency.

For Families

  • Tap SOS ‚Üí auto-detected location, emergency type, patient condition.
  • See nearest government + private options transparently, with ETA and hospital match.
  • Track ambulance in real-time; auto-SMS/WhatsApp to family/emergency contact.
  • Post-trip summary and digital record for continuity of care.

For Hospitals & Operators

  • Command dashboard with live fleet view and incident map.
  • Route suggestions based on real traffic and hospital load patterns.
  • Driver SOPs, checklists and KYC verification integrated.
  • Analytics on response times, peak hours and geography hotspots.

How ResQ Works End-to-End Flow

1. SOS Initiated User taps SOS / emergency card. App auto-captures GPS, asks 2‚Äì3 triage questions (e.g., conscious / unconscious).
2. Smart Matching Algorithm checks nearest government and private vehicles, plus suitable hospitals based on case type.
3. Driver Accepts Driver app surfaces high-priority requests first. Once accepted, route and timer start instantly.
4. Live Tracking & Alerts Family sees ambulance on map. SMS/WhatsApp link is shared with emergency contact and hospital.
5. Arrival & Handover On reaching hospital, trip is marked complete; a summary is logged in ResQ for quality measurement.

Safety & Trust Layer Non-Negotiable

In emergency tech, trust is as important as speed. ResQ‚Äôs design includes safeguards to reduce misuse, fraud and uncertainty.

Verification & Identity

  • Driver KYC and vehicle docs checked before activating on the network.
  • Visible driver profile, ambulance type and basic equipment in the consumer app.
  • Flags and internal review flows for suspicious patterns.

Communication & Transparency

  • Clear indication when a vehicle is government-linked vs private.
  • Live tracking links shareable with family and hospital teams.
  • Post-trip record of approximate time, route taken and status updates.

Responsible Design

  • Minimise unnecessary clicks in critical flows; only essential information is asked.
  • Fallback instructions (e.g., ‚Äúif app fails, call this number‚Äù) embedded in UX.
  • Scope for future integration with official helplines where permitted.
