Executive Summary Investor View

ResQ is an AI-first emergency response aggregation layer for India that connects government (108/102) and verified private ambulances on one real-time interface. The goal: cut urban response times by 30‚Äì50%, make pricing transparent, and give families and hospitals live visibility from ‚ÄúSOS‚Äù to ‚ÄúAdmitted‚Äù.

What ResQ does

A single mobile app and backend that: routes the nearest suitable ambulance, shows live ETA, notifies family, and keeps hospital teams ready before arrival.

Who it serves

Citizens in urban India, hospitals, ambulance operators, and over time government bodies that want data-driven response instead of manual dispatch.

Why now

Rising traffic, higher health awareness, and smartphones everywhere ‚Äì but emergency experience is still phone calls + guesswork. ResQ targets this gap.

Problem

No real-time visibility

Families and hospitals rarely get an accurate ETA. They rely on promises over calls, not live GPS ‚Äì increasing anxiety and risk.

Manual & fragmented dispatch

108/102 call centers and private operators mostly run manual allocation. This leads to wrong assignments, delays, or no-show scenarios.

Unclear pricing & trust deficit

Private ambulances often don‚Äôt show pricing in advance. Patients discover final costs only at the end, creating mistrust.

Traffic + no optimisation

Dense cities like Hyderabad lose critical minutes in traffic. There is very little predictive routing or smart coverage planning.

Solution

ResQ offers a simple emergency journey: SOS ‚Üí nearest verified ambulance ‚Üí live tracking ‚Üí cashless / clear billing ‚Üí digital record. For operators and hospitals, it becomes a lightweight control center without heavy hardware.

Core Features

  • One-tap SOS with current location locked
  • Nearest suitable ambulance auto-assigned (Govt / Private tag shown)
  • Live GPS + ETA + ambulance and driver details
  • Transparent rate-card per hospital & vehicle type
  • Automatic SMS to emergency contact when ambulance is assigned

Operational Modules

  • User app (SOS, hospital selection, tracking)
  • Driver app (accept, navigate, update status)
  • Admin dashboard (fleet view, SLAs, analytics)
  • APIs for hospitals & government dashboards
