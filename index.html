Problem ‚Äî Emergency in India is a lottery

For most Indian families, calling an ambulance is a gamble: no ETA, no visibility, no idea who is coming or how long it will take. Government lines are overloaded, private operators are fragmented, and traffic turns every minute into risk.

1. No real-time transparency

Families and hospitals rarely see live ambulance location or a reliable ETA. This uncertainty leads to panic, parallel calls, and sub-optimal hospital choices.

2. Manual, slow dispatch

Many control rooms still work with manual calls, paper logs and human judgement, with no algorithmic routing or auto-allocation to nearest capable unit.

3. Fragmented private fleet

Thousands of private ambulances operate outside any unified system. No single map of availability, no consistent quality checks, and no standard communication layer.

4. Traffic & route inefficiency

Dense corridors like Hyderabad‚Äôs IT belt see daily jams ‚Äî yet ambulances are not routed using real-time choke-point intelligence or past delay patterns.

Solution ‚Äî One pane of glass for emergency response

ResQ acts as a digital ‚Äúair traffic control‚Äù for ambulances and emergency responders. A single app + dashboard that connects citizens, control rooms, hospitals, and verified private fleets into one real-time, AI-assisted network.

Core Experience

  • One-tap SOS with automatic location detection (no manual typing).
  • Govt / Private toggle ‚Äî user or hospital can choose appropriate stream.
  • Live GPS map, ETA countdown, ambulance details and driver identity.
  • Automatic SMS to family / emergency contact with tracking link.

Operational Modules

  • User app: Request, track, share, rate.
  • Driver app: Accept, navigate via AI routing, update status.
  • Control dashboard: City view, dispatch, SLAs, bottlenecks.
  • Backend services: Real-time sync, messaging, event logs.

Design Principles

  • No vehicle ownership: asset-light, partner-first model.
  • Government-friendly: ability to plug into 108/102 flows.
  • Trust by design: KYC, ratings, incident logs and audit trails.
  • Simple UX: built for stressed users on budget smartphones.
