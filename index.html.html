Vision

  • Fix India's broken emergency response system with real-time tracking and transparency.
  • Create a single platform for ambulance, fire and rescue services.
  • Integrated government + private emergency vehicle ecosystem.

The Problem

  • No real-time emergency tracking in most cities.
  • 108/102 delays due to manual dispatching.
  • Unregulated private ambulance pricing.
  • Poor tech & no public-facing emergency platform.

Our Solution — One Emergency App

  • Instant booking of nearest emergency vehicle within 10 km.
  • Live GPS tracking + ETA + notifications.
  • Govt (free) & Private (paid) toggle.
  • Transparent pricing, verified providers.
  • Ratings, reviews, complete history & documentation.

Core App Modules (MVP)

User App
Book, track, contact driver, SOS
Driver App
Accept rides, navigate, update status
Admin Panel
Assign rides, verify partners, monitor realtime
Backend
Realtime sync, cloud messaging, GPS APIs

Why We'll Succeed

Strategy
  • Govt (free) & Private (paid) split model.
  • GPS tracking from day one.
  • KYC verified partners + ratings.
  • Pilot → Scale → Expand across cities.
Market
  • 40,000+ ambulances; only 10% digitized.
  • Huge gap in Tier‑2/3 cities.
  • Smart City + GovTech investment rising.

Business Model

  • Ride commissions (10–20%).
  • Hospital subscriptions.
  • Govt 108/102 integration contracts.
  • Corporate & NGO safety plans.
  • Future: insurance tie-ups, diagnostics transport.
